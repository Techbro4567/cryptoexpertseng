How does our service work?

You provide us with a copy of your private key backup (or Blockchain ID) and your best guesses as to what your password is. We will use your password guesses to “brute force” your password.

When We Can't Help

  • You have no idea what your password was
  • Your Password was 15+ randomly-chosen characters long

When we can help?

You have lost access to a common crypto asset, such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Dogecoin, an NFT or dozens of other alt coins

You thought you knew your password, but perhaps it involves different numbers or different capitalization than you remember

You used a handful of different passwords when you initially created the wallet, but you can't remember which one

You wrote down a list of words, and the passphrase is some combination of those word

You wrote down the password, but now it won't open the wallet

You have lost your crypto asset to scammers and you want to recover it
